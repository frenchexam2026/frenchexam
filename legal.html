Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how FrenchExam.ie, operated by Nicola Rowan, Carlow, Ireland, collects, uses and protects your personal data in accordance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and applicable Irish data protection law.

Who We Are

FrenchExam.ie is an online Higher Level Leaving Certificate French programme created and operated by Nicola Rowan, based in Carlow, Ireland.

Contact: lcfrenchworkshop@gmail.com

What Data We Collect

We collect the following personal data when you interact with FrenchExam.ie:

  • Your name and email address when you register interest in the programme
  • Your child's name and year group when you complete the registration form
  • Your email address when you sign up to receive the French Crimes guide or any other free resource

We use your personal data for the following purposes:

  • To contact you with details about the FrenchExam.ie programme ahead of the September 2026 launch
  • To send you the free resource you requested (e.g. the French Crimes guide)
  • To send you follow-up emails with French language tips and programme information
  • To analyse website traffic and improve our content via Google Analytics

Legal Basis for Processing

We process your personal data on the basis of your consent. You provide this consent when you tick the relevant checkbox on our registration or sign-up forms. You may withdraw your consent at any time by contacting us at lcfrenchworkshop@gmail.com or by clicking the unsubscribe link in any email we send you.

Email Marketing

If you sign up to receive our free resource or register interest in the programme, you will be added to our mailing list managed by MailerLite. You will receive a small number of follow-up emails with French language tips and information about the FrenchExam.ie programme.

You can unsubscribe from these emails at any time by clicking the unsubscribe link at the bottom of any email. We will never send you spam or share your email address with third parties for marketing purposes.

Third Party Services

We use the following third-party services which may process your data:

  • MailerLite — email marketing and automation. Their privacy policy is available at mailerlite.com
  • Google Analytics — website analytics. Data is anonymised and used only to understand how visitors use our site

Data Retention

We will retain your personal data for as long as necessary to fulfil the purposes for which it was collected, or for as long as you remain subscribed to our mailing list. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.

Your Rights

Under GDPR, you have the following rights:

  • The right to access the personal data we hold about you
  • The right to correct inaccurate personal data
  • The right to request deletion of your personal data
  • The right to withdraw consent at any time
  • The right to lodge a complaint with the Data Protection Commission (dataprotection.ie)

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at lcfrenchworkshop@gmail.com.

FrenchExam.ie is an online self-paced Higher Level Leaving Certificate French programme operated by Nicola Rowan, Carlow, Ireland.

Programme Description

FrenchExam.ie provides pre-recorded video lessons, notes and resources to support Higher Level Leaving Certificate French students. New lessons are released on a weekly basis from September through to May.

⚠️ This is a self-paced online programme. Madame Nicola does not provide individual feedback, corrections or responses to homework, essays, oral practice tasks or any other student work. The programme is designed for independent study alongside classroom learning.
